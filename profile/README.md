## About OneOff-Tech

[OneOff-Tech](https://oneofftech.de?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=about) helps organizations turn complex information into usable knowledge systems, open tools, and AI-supported workflows that fit their goals, constraints, and context.

Some of our recent activities:

- Transform unstructured data into enriched, [AI-ready datasets](https://oneofftech.de/blog/structured-extraction?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=blog-structured-extraction) for controlled deployment of RAG systems and LLMs.
- Turn knowledge into high-precision contexts for more [transparent and effective use](https://oneofftech.xyz/our-approach?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=knowledge-line) of Generative AI.
- Align tools, [governance](https://oneofftech.de/blog/introducing-the-knowledge-management-framework/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=blog-knowledge-management), and teams for [GDPR-compliant AI](https://oneofftech.de/blog/personal-data-ml?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=blog-personal-data-ml) facilitating stakeholder dialogue and accompanying leadership through digital transformation.
- Provide [open-source alternatives](https://oneofftech.de/blog/open-source-digital-platform-for-ithen-a-success-story/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=profile&utm_campaign=github-profile&utm_content=blog-open-source-ithen) to proprietary tools for EU-compliant, sovereign digital infrastructure.
